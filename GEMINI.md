## Project Overview

Big Bend land/probate tracking and pilot unit modeling program. Evidence-based property research and unit economics modeling.

## Research Scope

When performing research for The Burro:

- **Property Records:** County deed records, tax assessments
- **Probate Procedures:** Texas probate court requirements
- **Market Data:** Big Bend area land values, trends
- **Legal Research:** Texas property law, transfer requirements
- **Title:** Title search procedures, lien verification

## Source Validation

| Source Type | Trust Level |
|-------------|-------------|
| County clerk records | HIGH |
| Texas statutes | HIGH |
| Title company data | HIGH |
| Real estate listings | MEDIUM |
| Property forums | LOW (verify) |

## Domain Knowledge

**Texas Probate:** Informal vs formal administration, independent executor
**Property Transfer:** Deed types, recording requirements
**Big Bend Area:** Brewster, Presidio, Jeff Davis counties

## Guardrails

- NO real property addresses without consent
- Cite county/legal sources with dates
- Mark speculation vs confirmed facts
- Verify legal info against Texas statutes
- Preserve evidence chain of custody
