# Experiences & Workshops

## Overview

Big Bend Burro offers curated desert experiences that connect guests to the land, sky, and local artisan traditions. Focus is on what makes Big Bend unique: dark skies, remote wilderness, and hands-on creativity.

---

## Dark Sky Photography

### Workshop: Night Sky Immersion
| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Duration | 2-3 nights |
| Group Size | 4-8 participants |
| Skill Level | Beginner to Advanced |
| Price Range | $700-1,500/person |
| Season | Year-round (best Oct-Apr) |

**What's Included:**
- Instruction from professional astrophotographer
- Camera setup and settings for night sky
- Best viewing locations on property
- Post-processing basics
- Image review and feedback

**What Guests Need:**
- Camera with manual mode
- Tripod (can rent)
- Warm layers (nights are cold)
- Patience and wonder

**Premium Option:** Private instruction ($2,500-8,000)

### Why It Works Here
- Darkest skies in continental US (Bortle 1-2)
- 15,000 sq mi Dark Sky Reserve
- Clear skies 300+ nights/year
- No light pollution for miles

## Booking Flow

### Current Process
1. Guest inquires via website or assistant
2. Check availability across lodging and experiences
3. Create custom package or select preset
4. Waiver/consent forms sent
5. Payment through Lodgify/Stripe
6. Confirmation with itinerary

### What to Confirm
- Dates and party size
- Skill/fitness level
- Equipment needs (cameras, etc.)
- Dietary requirements (if meals included)
- Transportation (self-drive or shuttle needed)

## FAQ

**Q: I've never done photography - can I do the workshop?**
A: Absolutely. We start from basics. Just bring a camera with manual mode.

**Q: Is the river trip safe for kids?**
A: Yes, calm sections are family-friendly. Check water level first.

**Q: What if it's cloudy for dark sky viewing?**
A: We can reschedule or adjust to terrestrial photography. Weather happens.

**Q: Can we combine multiple experiences?**
A: Yes - that's what packages are for. We'll build something custom.
