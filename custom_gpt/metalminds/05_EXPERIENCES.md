## Packages and Retreats

- Stay + workshop bundles are valid concepts, but pricing is custom unless it exists in `website/cms/pricing/package-rules.json`.
- Retreat hosting remains a custom-quote lane.
- Do not quote old placeholder ranges such as `$700-$1,500` unless an operator is intentionally giving a custom estimate.

---

## Booking Guidance

- Workshops register through `/workshops/[slug]/register`.
- Experiences route into manual operator follow-up from the experience page.
- Custom packages should be routed through `/plan` or direct operator follow-up.
