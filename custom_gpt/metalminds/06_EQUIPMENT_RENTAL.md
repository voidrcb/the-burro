# Equipment Rental Operations

## Overview

Big Bend Burro rents construction and ranch equipment to local contractors, ranchers, and property owners in Brewster County. This is a serious revenue stream in an area where equipment is scarce and delivery costs are high.

## Rental Requirements

### Operator Qualifications
- Valid driver's license
- Equipment operation experience (self-certified)

- Insurance required (renters or add-on)

### Documentation Required
1. Signed rental agreement
2. Damage waiver acknowledgment
3. Insurance verification
4. Credit card authorization (damage deposit)

## Inspection Protocol

### Pre-Rental Checklist
- [ ] Walk-around visual inspection
- [ ] Fluid levels (hydraulic, engine oil, coolant)
- [ ] Track/tire condition
- [ ] Lights and safety features
- [ ] Controls function test
- [ ] Hour meter reading
- [ ] Photo documentation
- [ ] Fuel level

### Post-Rental Checklist
- [ ] Walk-around inspection
- [ ] Check for damage
- [ ] Fluid levels
- [ ] Hour meter reading
- [ ] Fuel level (return full policy)
- [ ] Photo documentation
- [ ] Note any issues

### Documentation
- Photos at pickup and return
- Written notes on condition
- Signed by both parties

## Pricing Strategy

### Market Context
- Nearest equipment rental is Alpine (100 mi) or Midland (200+ mi)
- Delivery costs often exceed rental cost
- Local availability is a significant advantage
- Premium pricing justified by convenience

### Competitive Position
- 30-40% below major rental chains
- Significantly cheaper than delivery from Alpine
- Personal service and flexibility

### Revenue Targets
- Equipment rental is consistent income year-round
- Not weather-dependent like lodging
- Targets local contractors and ranchers

## Liability Considerations

### What We Require
- Signed waiver acknowledging risks
- Damage deposit authorization
- Insurance verification
- Competence self-certification

### What We Don't Do
- Operate equipment for renters
- Train inexperienced operators
- Guarantee equipment availability (reserve ahead)
- Cover renter's work or injuries

### Escalation
- Any damage: Document, photograph, report immediately
- Any injury: Emergency services first, then document
- Any dispute: Escalate to RCB

## FAQ

**Q: Do you deliver?**
A: Yes, within 50 miles. Customer pickup available if you have a trailer.

**Q: Do I need experience?**
A: Yes. We don't provide training. Operators must be competent.

**Q: What if something breaks?**
A: Contact us immediately. Normal wear is expected; damage from misuse is renter responsibility.

**Q: Can I rent by the hour?**
A: Minimum is half-day (4 hours).

**Q: What about fuel?**
A: Return with full tank or pay fuel surcharge.

## Steel Buildings Service

### Overview

Chuck helps local ranchers and businesses source commercial steel buildings. This is a consultation and brokering service - we don't manufacture buildings but help customers find the right solution.

### Why Steel for Big Bend

- **Desert durability**: Withstands extreme heat, UV, and dust storms
- **Wind resistance**: Rated for high wind loads common in open terrain
- **Low maintenance**: No rotting, warping, or termites
- **Fast construction**: Pre-engineered components reduce build time

### Common Applications

| Application | Typical Size | Notes |
|-------------|--------------|-------|
| Ranch workshops | 30x40 to 50x60 | Equipment maintenance, welding |
| Equipment storage | 24x30 to 40x60 | Vehicle and machinery protection |
| Agricultural buildings | 40x60 to 60x100 | Hay barns, livestock shelters |
| Commercial facilities | Variable | Retail, offices, mixed-use |
| Residential garages | 24x24 to 30x40 | Oversized garages, hobby shops |
| Event spaces | 40x60+ | Covered venues, markets |

### Process

1. **Consultation**: Discuss needs, budget, timeline
2. **Site assessment**: Review location, access, foundation requirements
3. **Quote**: Work with manufacturers for competitive pricing
4. **Coordination**: Help coordinate delivery and erector connections

### Service Area

Primarily Brewster County and greater Big Bend region:
- Terlingua
- Study Butte
- Alpine
- Marathon
- Surrounding ranch properties
