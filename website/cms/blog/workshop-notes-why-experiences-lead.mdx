The artisan side of Big Bend Burro is not being designed around shipping the heaviest and most fragile work to strangers first.

Experiences travel better than freight. Workshops also let people understand the materials, the land, and the rhythm behind the objects. That is a stronger first relationship than a catalog full of fragile promises.

The public site will keep telling that story as new workshop formats take shape.
