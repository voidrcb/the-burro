The first pilot decisions are not about maximum capacity. They are about whether a one-unit path can be run responsibly.

Every future stay, workshop, and night-sky experience depends on infrastructure that respects the limits of the site. That is why water, wastewater, and access stay in front of expansion.

We would rather explain the constraint honestly than build a version of Burro that ignores it.
